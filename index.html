Favorite Websites

Below is a list of websites I like to visit often. Mostly they are for entertainment, but a couple of them have educational value as well.

Website Explanation
Reddit Reddit is a social media website where you can make and see posts in specific mini-communities called subreddits. It's a great way to find more people who like your interests, as well as reading some great stories.
YouTube YouTube is a video-viewing website where creators can post short-, medium-, or long-form content. It's a great place to watch clips from your favorite shows, learn from educational creators, or follow some of your favorite online stars.
Edabit Edabit is website where you can practice coding challenges in various languages, including Java, C#, and Javascript. I like to use it to stay in practice with the languages I know, mainly Java. It also includes basic tutorials in Javascript and Python.
WordReference WordReference is a website that let's you translate words from one language to another. It also gives you their grammatical conjugations in various languages and example sentences so you can see the different contexts in which they are used. It is a very helpful tool for me while I'm learning French.
Coolmath Games Coolmath Games is a website where you can play challenging online games. There are many different types of games, such as Match 3 games, business games, and platformer games. I played it more when I was younger, but even now I enjoy playing some games from time to time.
